## §1 Charter

The CFPE Paradigm Forecast is a **scenario-tested Bayesian cascading dependency model** spanning four eras of quantum computing and fundamental physics (2026–2126+). It was systematically attacked from eight independent directions across five execution phases and converged on a definitive cascade expected value of **6.5% [4–9%]**.

## §1.2 Core Claim

> *The full paradigm-shift cascade (FCI topological QC → bio-spintronics → hydrodynamic quantum foundations → adelic physics with geometric constants) has a ≤9% probability of realization, with continuity (incremental gate-based QC progression) being the dominant scenario across all four eras at ≥82% per era.*

**Falsification conditions:**
- CMB-S4 detects log-periodic oscillations at >5σ → Eras 3-4 EV ↑ 3-5×
- CMB-S4 null result (exclusion at >5σ) → Eras 3-4 collapse
- Gate-based QC scales past 10k qubits without thermal issues → Era 1 FCI thesis falsified
- Any Standard Model constant derived from pure geometry to <1% precision → Era 4 validated

**Calibration labels:** Main claim `[speculative]` — based on Bayesian cascade model with systematic adversarial testing. Individual sub-claims carry evidence grades (DIRECT/INDIRECT/THEORETICAL/SPECULATIVE). Falsifiability conditions specified above.
